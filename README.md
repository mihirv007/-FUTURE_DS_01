## Key Insights

### 1. Revenue by Country
The United Kingdom generates the highest revenue (~8.18M), dominating the overall sales performance. Other countries such as Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, and France contribute significantly smaller portions of revenue.

### 2. Average Order Value
The Netherlands has the highest average order value, indicating customers tend to place larger purchases per transaction.

### 3. Seasonal Revenue Trends
Revenue increases significantly toward the end of the year. November records the highest monthly revenue, suggesting strong seasonal demand.

### 4. Top Revenue Products
The highest revenue-generating product is DOTCOM POSTAGE, followed by products such as Regency Cake Stand and White Hanging Heart.

### 5. Quantity vs Revenue
The product WORLD WAR 2 GLIDERS has the highest quantity sold but does not generate the highest revenue. This shows that high sales volume does not always result in the highest revenue.

### 6. Customer Distribution
A large portion of revenue comes from a relatively small group of highly active customers.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions like the United Kingdom.
- Promote high-revenue products to increase profitability.
- Prepare inventory for seasonal demand spikes toward the end of the year.
- Identify and retain high-value customers.
